Veggie Sushi


Decription

This vegetarian sweet potato tempura sushi roll is the perfect recipe for a date night in! It’s easy, delicious, and can be made so many different ways. It’s the perfect gluten free meal prep recipe because it stores well and can be eaten on the go!

Ingredients

  • Nori
  • Sushi rice
  • Sweet potato
  • Avocado
  • Cucumber
  • Sea salt
  • Tahini dressing

Steps

  1. Lay down the nori sheet: Add your nori sheet to a bamboo mat, rough side up.
  2. Create the base: Add the cooled rice to the nori, and press down flat.
  3. Add your fillings: Add sweet potato, avocado and cucumber to the bottom.
  4. Roll the ingredients: Lift the bottom, tuck the filling in and roll until the end.
  5. Cut into servings: Use a very sharp knife and cut into 6 to 8 pieces.
  6. Serve: Serve with a bit of sriracha and enjoy!
