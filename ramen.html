Ramen Noodles


Decription

A delicious bowl of ramen noodles containing ramen, soft boiled eggs, tofu, teriyaki seasoned chicken and a wide variety of vegetables! Using a miso stock this bowl will provide you with plenty of the savory and salty taste that ramen noodles are known and loved for. The best part? It's incredibly easy to make!

Ingredients

  • Ramen
  • Green Onion, Garlic & Ginger
  • Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Honey
  • 250 grams of chicken breast
  • Chili Garlic Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles: Bring water to a boil according to package instructions.
  2. Saute: Add the onion, garlic, ginger and oil to a small pan and saute for 5 minutes.
  3. Make sauce: Add in all remaining ingredients and whisk well, simmer over low heat for 5 minutes.
  4. Combine: Combine the cooked noodles with the sauce and let it simmer for a few minutes.
  5. Taste: Taste the dish and adjust flavors per your preference.
  6. Serve: Serve with fresh green onion and red pepper flakes.
