Thai Red Curry


Decription

Ready in just 15 minutes, these Thai Red Curry Noodles are the perfect simple dinner recipe. Theyâ€™re naturally vegan, gluten free and pair perfectly with steamed veggies and your choice of protein!

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Coconut Milk
  • Salt
  • Curry & Tomato Paste
  • Soy Sauce
  • Honey & Lime
  • Garlic & Ginger

Steps

  1. Prepare the skillet: Add minced garlic, grated ginger and olive oil. Saute until brown.
  2. Add the coconut milk: Add the coconut milk to the mix, preferably full fat and canned.
  3. Bring to a boil: Boil, whisk in the corn starch, then reduce heat and let simmer for 3 minutes.
  4. Combine: Combine the sauces and noodles and simmer.
  5. Garnish: Garnish with cilantro, scallions and red pepper flakes.
