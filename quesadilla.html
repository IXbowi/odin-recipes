Sweet potato quesadillas


Decription

These sweet potato and black bean vegan quesadillas are hearty, filling and packed with flavor. Serve them with my homemade cashew queso for a total crowd pleasing and easy dinner!

Ingredients

  • Sweet potatoes
  • Onion
  • Oil
  • Black Beans
  • Chipotle powder
  • Paprika & Cumin
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook potatoes: Peel and cube the sweet potatoes and add them to a pot. Cover and boil until tender
  2. Mix ingredients: Add diced onion, sauted for 3 minutes, to the mix. Add beans, powder, salt and pepper and paprika.
  3. Heat tortillas: Heat tortillas on a greased skillet, add potato filling.
  4. Cook: Fold the tortilla in half and add to skillet. Cook both sides for 2 minutes.
  5. Serve: Serve with guacamole or cashew queso.
